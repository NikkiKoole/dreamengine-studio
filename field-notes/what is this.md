DreamEngine Research Journal

This folder is not documentation.

It is not a design specification.

It is not a collection of decisions.

It is a research journal.

Its purpose is to capture changes in our understanding of DreamEngine as the project evolves.

Why this exists

DreamEngine changes continuously.

New carts are built.

Ideas are abandoned.

The North Star evolves.

Workflows improve.

A traditional design document quickly becomes outdated.

Instead of trying to describe DreamEngine as a finished system, this journal records the discoveries that changed our understanding of it.

The emphasis is on learning, not permanence.

Field Notes

Field notes are small observations that earned promotion.

A field note is written when something changes how we think about the project.

Not every interesting idea deserves a field note.

The observation should survive discussion and be supported by evidence from the repository, experiments or experience.

Field notes are intentionally small.

Each should capture a single insight.

Syntheses

Synthesis notes combine multiple field notes into larger working theories.

Unlike field notes, syntheses are expected to evolve.

They should be treated as research rather than truth.

A living notebook

This journal is intentionally append-only.

Small corrections are encouraged.

Major changes in understanding should not overwrite earlier notes.

Instead, write a new note.

The evolution of understanding is often as valuable as the understanding itself.

Confidence

Each note records its confidence.

Observed

Supported by repeated evidence.

Hypothesis

A promising explanation that requires more evidence.

Working Theory

A synthesis that currently explains multiple observations but remains open to revision.

Confidence may increase over time.

It may also decrease.

Both outcomes are valuable.

The Promotion Principle

DreamEngine promotes ideas only after they repeatedly prove themselves.

This journal follows the same philosophy.

Interesting thoughts remain conversations.

Repeated observations become field notes.

Repeated field notes become syntheses.

Nothing becomes permanent simply because it sounds compelling.

The role of this journal

This folder exists to help future readers understand how DreamEngine became what it is, not merely what it contains.

Software evolves.

Understanding evolves.

This journal attempts to preserve the latter.

For future contributors

Do not ask:

"What should I add?"

Instead ask:

"What changed our understanding?"

That is usually where the next field note begins.

The goal is not to produce more notes.

The goal is to notice when reality teaches us something we did not previously know.

Those moments deserve to be preserved.
